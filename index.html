Welcome

As your Shelby County Register of Deeds, I am pleased to provide this additional service to our customers. With this map function our customers can search by address, owner, or parcel number any property in Shelby County. Results will provide an aerial view of the property, surrounding parcels, assessment information, sales data, deeds, and property tax information for Shelby County as well as the muncipality in which the parcel is located. We welcome questions, comments, and concerns at register@shelbycountytn.gov. As with all of our web services, “Excellent customer service and care is our priority.”
